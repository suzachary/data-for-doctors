About Us

Our Mission:
In today's world, many people suffer from various chronic diseases such as diabetes or heart issues. These can all culminate in severe reactions such as heart attacks. In this project, we worked on identifying regions that had the highest frequency of these issues as well as finding factors to assist doctors. In turn, we are helping to promote health care in America today.
